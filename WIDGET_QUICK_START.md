# 📱 Home Screen Widgets - Quick Start

Get instant access to your daily verse and reading streak right from your home screen!

## What Are Home Screen Widgets?

Widgets are small app components that display information on your phone's home screen without opening the full app. SanctuaryFlow now includes two widgets to help you stay connected to your spiritual growth:

### 1. 📖 Daily Verse Widget
Displays today's inspirational Bible verse with:
- Full verse text
- Scripture reference
- Refresh button
- Tap to open app

### 2. 🔥 Streak Counter Widget
Shows your reading progress with:
- Current streak count
- Best (longest) streak
- Quick action buttons
- Visual indicator for today's reading

## Adding Widgets to Your Home Screen

### 📱 Android (5 Simple Steps)

1. **Long press** on an empty area of your home screen
2. Tap **"Widgets"** from the menu
3. Scroll to find **"SanctuaryFlow"**
4. **Drag** the widget you want (Daily Verse or Reading Streak) to your home screen
5. **Resize** if needed by dragging the corners

### 🍎 iOS (4 Simple Steps)

1. **Long press** on an empty area of your home screen
2. Tap the **"+"** icon in the top-left corner
3. Search for **"SanctuaryFlow"** and select it
4. Choose your widget (**Daily Verse** or **Reading Streak**) and tap **"Add Widget"**

## Widget Features

### Daily Verse Widget

**What It Shows:**
- A new Bible verse each day (changes automatically)
- The scripture reference (e.g., "Proverbs 3:5-6")

**What You Can Do:**
- Tap the **refresh icon** to update the verse
- Tap **anywhere on the widget** to open the app and read more

**Updates:**
- Automatically refreshes every hour
- Updates when you open the app
- Changes to a new verse each day

### Streak Counter Widget

**What It Shows:**
- Your **current reading streak** (in days)
- Your **best streak** (personal record)
- A **check mark** ✓ when you've read today
- Quick action buttons for **Prayer** and **Reading Plans**

**What You Can Do:**
- Tap the **widget** to view your full Bible tracker
- Tap **Prayer** button to open Prayer Schedule
- Tap **Reading** button to open Reading Plans

**Updates:**
- Automatically refreshes every hour
- Updates immediately when you complete a reading
- Resets daily at midnight

## Tips for Best Experience

✅ **Keep Them Visible**: Place widgets on your main home screen for daily inspiration

✅ **Use Both**: Add both widgets to get verse inspiration AND track progress

✅ **Quick Actions**: Use the streak widget's buttons for fast access to Prayer and Reading

✅ **Size Matters**: 
- Daily Verse works best in **medium** or **large** size
- Streak Counter works best in **medium** size

✅ **Stay Updated**: Open the app occasionally to ensure widgets have the latest data

## Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Updating?

**Try these solutions:**

1. **Tap the widget** to open the app (this refreshes the data)
2. **Remove and re-add** the widget
3. **Restart your phone**
4. **Check app permissions** in Settings

### Widget Shows Old Data?

- Open the SanctuaryFlow app
- The widgets will automatically update with current data
- Widgets refresh every hour when your phone is unlocked

### Can't Find Widgets? (iOS)

- Make sure you've opened the app at least once
- Check that iOS is updated to the latest version
- Try restarting your phone

### Widget Not Responding to Taps? (Android)

- Try removing and re-adding the widget
- Ensure the app is updated to the latest version
- Clear the app cache in Settings

## Privacy & Battery

🔒 **Privacy**: All widget data is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

🔋 **Battery**: Widgets are optimized to minimize battery usage. They only update once per hour and don't run continuously.

📊 **Data**: Widgets use minimal data and storage space.
